Core Features

Everything you need to
rule the battlefield.

No other mod goes this deep. Statify pulls server history logs, cross-references UUID data, and reconstructs your entire PvP career from day one.

🕰️
Retroactive History Engine
Install today, see your stats from day one. Statify's patented history engine scans server logs and UUID records to reconstruct kills, deaths, and match data - even years before installation.
Core Feature
📊
Live HUD Overlay
A sleek in-game HUD shows real-time K/D, current streak, and session kills. Fully customizable position, opacity, and color. Zero performance impact with async rendering.
Display
🏆
Global ELO Ranking
Compete on a worldwide leaderboard powered by a custom ELO algorithm. Rankings factor in opponent difficulty, server population, and win streaks for the most accurate skill rating system in Minecraft.
Competitive
⚔️
Combat Analytics
Deep-dive stats: weapon preference breakdown, time-of-day performance, favorite biomes to fight in, kill distance tracking, and critical hit ratios. Know yourself like never before.
Analytics
👥
Rival Tracker
Automatically detects players you've killed or been killed by most. Track your nemesis head-to-head ratio, compare stats, and get notified when they join your server.
Social
🔗
Multi-Server Sync
Your stats follow you across every server. Statify syncs data via a lightweight background service, merging stats from Hypixel, Mineplex, private SMPs - all into one unified profile.
Cross-Server
Installation

Up and running in
4 steps.

01
📦
Download the Mod
Grab the latest .jar from the download section. Requires the Fabric loader for MC 1.21+.
02
📂
Drop in Mods Folder
Place the .jar file in your .minecraft/mods folder. No additional config needed - plug and play.
03
🚀
Launch
Fire up Minecraft. Statify auto-authenticates with your Mojang UUID and begins the background history scan immediately.
04
📈
Watch History Build
Within minutes, your all-time stats appear. The history engine continues scanning in the background - even recovering data from years ago.
